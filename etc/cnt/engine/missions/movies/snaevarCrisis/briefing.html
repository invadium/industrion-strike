Snaevar Crisis

Snaevar is a star system claimed by Industrion. It doesn't have any valuable resources or habitable planets. But there are tunnels leading directly to Magdalena Crossway. That makes Snaevar a key system in controling the trade routes.

Responding on increasing pirates activity in the region, Dominion sent task force into N-9412. Dominion announced that task force's goal is to secure navigation in the system.

As response to increasing presence of Dominion forces in the Magdalena region, Industrion starts establishing a military base in the Snaevar star system.

Diplomatic solutions failed.
