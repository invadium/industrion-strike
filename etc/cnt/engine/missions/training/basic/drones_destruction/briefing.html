Basic Training: Intersept Drones

It's time to learn flight controls and missile controls.

Destroy 3 enemy drones before they reach their destination - Beacon D.

You have 2 Stinger missiles loaded. Select target with Space key or by clicking on the radar mark. Use V key to launch a missile. You could also launch a missile with camera mounted to it, so you could observe how it will hit the target. Use B key for that. Note, that it takes some time to reload the missile. The time remaining displayed on the RCH indicator.

Tactics here is following. You select first drone and kill it by a missile. Then accelerate with A key and finish second drone by lasers. By that time missile launcher should be recharged - target final drone and kill it by a missile.
